How to Open an NRE Account

An NRE (Non-Resident External) account is an essential financial tool for NRIs to manage their income earned abroad. Here’s a step-by-step guide to opening an NRE account in India:

  1. Choose a bank in India that offers NRE accounts.
  2. Gather the necessary documents: passport, visa, overseas address proof, and passport-sized photographs.
  3. Submit the account opening form along with the documents at the chosen bank’s branch or online.
  4. Fund the account with foreign currency, which will be converted to INR.
  5. Once the account is active, you can repatriate funds freely and earn tax-free interest.
